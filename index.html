Current and Past Projects and Research

Research with Dr. Zhewei Sun(TTIC)

My current research focuses on developing NLP techniques to better process and model informal language across languages

We are collecting slang datasets and using state-of-the-art sentence embedding techniques to enhance automatic processing of slang across a variety of languages.

Language Learning App

This app uses a fine-tuned GPT-4o model and a Spaced-Repition-System algorithm to hack the brain to best remember vocabulary.

The GPT model has been fine tuned to produce text in a number of registers, from academic to conversational.

Currently I am working on making the app more presentable, capable of handling multiple users, and producing stranger sentences(to maximize retention)

It is currently backseated due to the ongoing research, but my progress is linked below!
